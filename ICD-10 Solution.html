ICD-10 Solution

ICD10: The raising Billion Dollar Baby

On October 1, 2014 a key element of the dataset of the USA health care system will undergo a major transformation. The bill for ICD-10 implementation is passed a long back and finally the mandate implementation date would be Oct 1, 2014. The transition will impact most of the USA Healthcare segment including Payers, Providers, Medical Coding and Billing company etc. Apart from ICD-10, meticulous compliance adherence (ARRA, HITECH), documentation changes (from 4010 to 5010), ACA will also increase the burden on the healthcare practices. For the time being it may be optional; however to sustain the business excellence we must get ready to the healthcare transformation.

ICD10: The big Question of Sustainability

In order to sustain the futuristic need of healthcare reform, the Healthcare stakeholder should amend the data competency with the ICD-10. The necessary tedious task includes EMR integration with ICD-10 dataset, Revenue optimization, Pre-testing of the claim processing etc.

The increased pressure for the key transformation unlocked the hidden business opportunities throughout the USA healthcare dimension. The innovative changes will demand high volume of skilled professional.

ICD-10 Implementation: Scope of Work

As an organization we provide end to end ICD-10 solution and services to run and sustain the current and futuristic need of ICD-10 implementation, electronic database and software application, EMR, claim engine etc. As an organization we continuously work to conquer the solution to optimize and maximize business profitability.
