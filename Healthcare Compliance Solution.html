Healthcare Compliance Solution

As Healthcare services utilization is expanding every day, the chance of health care fraud and abuse is growing exponentially. The US federal and OIG (office of inspector general) is increasing the interest to control the fraud and abuse. One major step has been taken back in 2009 as implementation of ARRA, HITECH, and ACA. Also to increase the documentation and ease data capturing ICD-10 and 5010 were proposed. In current dynamic scenario, practices that don’t implement compliance programs could be putting themselves at risk. So in the nutshell it is advisable to amend the compliance program and internal audit before the knock of RAC/MAC etc…

Here we can assist you with our expert solution to develop and amend the compliance program and Internal audit.


Targeted Services that require careful attention for audits are:

  •  Inpatient services
  •  Visits that combine E/M services and procedures
  •  Nursing home visits
  •  Home health care supervision
  •  X-rays or ECGs
  •  Laboratory services
  •  Sleep Medicine
