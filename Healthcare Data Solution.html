Healthcare Data Solution

On October 1, 2015 a key element of the dataset of the USA health care system went into a major transformation. The transition will impact most of the USA Healthcare segment including Payers, Providers, Medical Coding and Billing company etc.

In order to sustain the futuristic need of healthcare reform, the Healthcare stakeholder should amend the data competency with the ICD-10. The necessary tedious task includes EMR integration with ICD-10 dataset, Revenue optimization, Pre-testing of the claim processing etc.

We provide potential data solution and services to run and sustain the current and futuristic need of your electronic database, software, EMR, claim engine etc. As an organization we continuously work to conquer the solution to optimize and maximize business needs.

Key Highlights




  •  Data available for Provider and payer edition.
  •  Real time data update (TAT-24 hrs).
  •  Implemented Clinical documentation logic.
  •  Annual update for 1 year.
  •  Includes reverse crosswalk (for crosswalk dataset).
  •  Different format available (PDF, MS-CLS, Notepad) to integrate with EMR, claim engine.
  •  Built in with Special Logics for revenue optimization.
  •  On-line, On-call support.
